# Malaria Clinical Trials in Kenya - Summary

## Phase Distribution
According to ClinicalTrials.gov analysis, there are 121 clinical trials for malaria in Kenya:
- PHASE2: 33 studies
- PHASE3: 30 studies
- PHASE4: 18 studies
- PHASE1: 10 studies
- Not Applicable: 25 studies
- Unknown: 14 studies

## WHO Context
In 2024, the WHO African Region was home to 95% of malaria cases (265 million) and 95% (579,000) of malaria deaths.
